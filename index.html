Team Allocation

Each of the 8 players was randomly assigned 8 teams from the full 64-team field. Every team belongs to exactly one player. Draw was completely random.

The Spread Rule — Core Mechanic

Every day at 9:00 AM EST, spreads lock from DraftKings.com. Example: Duke -29.5 means Duke must win by more than 29.5 to cover.

📦 Favorite wins but does NOT cover:
The losing team's owner acquires the winning team from their owner. Cover transfer!
✅ Favorite wins and covers:
Clean win. No transfer. Both teams stay with their owners (loser eliminated).
✅ Underdog wins outright (upset):
Underdog just advances normally. No transfer — owner keeps their team.
❌ Team eliminated:
Stayed on your card crossed out so everyone can see your history.
Winning the Pool

Pool runs through the National Championship on April 6, 2026 in Indianapolis. Player who owns the winning team at the final buzzer wins. Teams can change hands multiple times.
